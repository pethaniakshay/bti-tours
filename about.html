About Us

we are bold or italic, never regular

A journey which started with a dream. A Dream to travel the world, A Dream to explore the unexplored, A Dream to see this beautiful world created by master himself, A Dream to extend the boundaries created by our own mind, A Dream to live life like never before. We are following our dreams.

For us BTI is not only profession but it is our passion. We are here to serve you. We are here to make your dreams a beautiful reality.

We assure you the best service, flexibility, safety and comfort. Your satisfaction is our goal. We have budget tours as well as luxuries you want. Best price in the market we guarantee.

Anywhere in the world we have the network to plan your trip. We know what your trip means to you. It is once in a life experience. So we will make it memorable as you dreamt. So what are you waiting for!!! Pick up your phone and make your dream trip a beautiful experience…

It’s never really work at here as we love and enjoy doing what we do and turn the impossible into the possible. We value honesty, hard-work, transparency, and respect each other. We work seriously while not taking ourselves too seriously.
